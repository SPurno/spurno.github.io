SPurno Animation
Studio

A motion graphics and stock video animation studio creating premium assets for creators worldwide.

Our Story

Bringing Visions to Life

SPurno Animation Studio was founded by Faruk Ahmed — a Web Developer, Motion Graphics Designer, and Animation Creator based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. What started as a passion for visual storytelling has grown into a studio serving creators in over 100 countries.

We specialise in premium motion background videos, animated templates, and stock footage — with over 4,000 assets available on Adobe Stock, Shutterstock, Pond5, and Freepik.

Our Journey

Growth & Milestones

From a single animation to a global studio.

2018

Studio Founded

Faruk Ahmed starts creating motion graphics as a passion project, releasing first assets on Adobe Stock.

2019

Multi-Platform Expansion

Expanded to Shutterstock and Pond5, reaching creators across 50+ countries.

2021

1,000+ Assets Milestone

Reached 1,000 premium assets with a focus on 4K quality and broadcast-ready templates.

2023

4,000+ Assets & Freepik

Surpassed 4,000 assets across all platforms. Joined Freepik to expand reach further.

2026

Global Studio

Serving creators in 100+ countries with motion graphics, infographics, broadcast packages, and custom animation work.
