Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SPurno Animation Studio handles information connected with this website, contact requests, analytics, advertising, and links to stock marketplaces.

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Information We May Collect

We may receive information that visitors choose to send through contact forms, email, or social links, such as a name, email address, project details, and message content. Standard website logs may also include browser type, device information, referring pages, approximate location, and pages viewed.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to respond to inquiries, maintain the website, understand which content is useful, improve motion graphics resources, protect the site from abuse, and support advertising or analytics features.

Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

This site may use cookies, analytics tools, and advertising services such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to measure traffic, personalize ads where allowed, and report performance. Visitors can manage cookies through browser settings and applicable ad preference tools.

Third-Party Links

SPurno Animation Studio links to platforms including Adobe Stock, Shutterstock, Pond5, Freepik, YouTube, and social media websites. Those websites operate under their own privacy policies, licensing rules, and account terms.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal contact messages. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate hosting, analytics, email, advertising, security, or marketplace links, and may be disclosed when required by law or needed to protect the website.

Your Choices

You can avoid sending contact information, disable cookies in your browser, use privacy controls provided by advertising networks, or contact SPurno Animation Studio about a previous message.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact SPurno Animation Studio through the website contact page or official social profiles linked from this site.
