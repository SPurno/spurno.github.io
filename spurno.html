About

Passionate about motion & code

I am a Multimedia & Graphics professional with 7+ years of cross-industry experience working in architectural firms, e-commerce, and software development. I founded SPurno Animation Studio to create premium motion assets for creators worldwide.

My work spans motion graphics, infographic animation, product mockups, presentation templates, and advertising visuals — all designed to help creators and brands publish polished content faster. Every asset is built with clean composition, clear movement, and broad usability in mind.

Beyond stock content, I offer custom animation services and web development solutions, delivering broadcast-quality motion graphics that meet international production standards. I specialize in Adobe After Effects and have experience in web development, Android development, and digital marketing.
