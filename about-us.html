About us

A focused animation studio for reusable visual content.

SPurno Animation Studio produces motion graphics assets that are practical, flexible, and ready for real campaigns. Our work supports creators, agencies, marketers, educators, and product teams with animated backgrounds, presentation visuals, device mockups, sales banners, typography animations, and infographic templates.

Every asset is designed with clean composition, clear movement, and broad usability in mind, so buyers can adapt it for websites, ads, social media, YouTube, presentations, and commercial video projects.

Work process

A clear workflow keeps every project usable and on brand.

Whether we are preparing stock footage or custom motion graphics, the process stays practical: define the use case, design the system, animate with restraint, and deliver files that are easy to publish.

Creative Brief

We clarify the audience, platform, format, duration, and required visual direction before production begins.

Design Direction

Layouts, color, hierarchy, and motion rhythm are planned so the final animation feels intentional.

Animation Build

Assets are animated, rendered, checked for timing, and prepared for stock or client delivery.

Delivery

Final files are exported in the requested format with practical naming and revision support when needed.
