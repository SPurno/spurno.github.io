FAQs

Quick answers to common questions about our services, formats, and policies.

What file formats are included?

All assets come in industry-standard formats. Video clips are provided as MP4/H.264 and ProRes 422 in 4K resolution. Motion graphics templates are available in .aep (After Effects), .mogrt (Premiere Pro), and .psd (Photoshop). Digital assets include AI, EPS, PNG, and SVG formats.

How does the instant download work?

After your payment is verified (typically within a few hours for bank transfers, instantly for digital wallets), you receive a secure download link via email. You can also access all purchased files from your profile dashboard under My Orders — no expiration, no limits.

Can I use these assets for commercial projects?

Yes! All assets come with a Standard License that covers commercial use in client projects, YouTube videos, social media content, advertising, broadcast, and film.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Payoneer and Skrill for manual payment processing. Simply send the total amount to our designated account and submit the Transaction ID during checkout.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If an asset has a technical issue (corrupted file, wrong format, playback error), we will provide a replacement or full refund.

Do you offer custom animation services?

Yes! We provide custom motion graphics and animation services for brands, agencies, and content creators. Reach out through our Contact page with your project brief.
