Infographic template downloads give you instant access to professional motion graphics assets. Whether you need animated charts, icon packs, or full infographic scenes, these templates are ready to use in After Effects.

Why Download Infographic Templates

Downloading infographic templates saves you hours of production time while delivering professional-quality results. Professional templates come with pre-animated elements, organized layer structures, and customizable controls that let you create stunning infographics without advanced motion design skills. In today's competitive content landscape, businesses and creators who leverage data visualization outperform those who rely on static graphics alone.

Infographic templates are particularly valuable for content marketing strategies. Research shows that content with relevant infographics receives 94% more views than content without visual data presentation. Animated infographics take this engagement even further by adding motion that captures attention and guides viewers through data narratives step by step. With SPurno's templates, you can produce broadcast-quality animated infographics in a fraction of the time it would take to build them from scratch.

All templates available through SPurno Animation Studio are compatible with Adobe After Effects and include detailed documentation for quick setup. Each template is built with clean expressions and efficient rendering in mind, ensuring smooth performance even on moderately equipped workstations. We optimize every template for the best balance of visual quality and render speed.

The SPurno template library covers a wide spectrum of infographic styles. Whether you need corporate data presentations with clean bar charts, energetic social media infographics with bold typography, or educational content with animated diagrams, you will find templates designed specifically for each use case. Each template undergoes rigorous testing before release to ensure compatibility with After Effects CC 2020 and newer versions.

What You Get

  • Fully animated infographic scenes with editable text and colors
  • Modular chart components — bars, pies, lines, and number counters
  • Icon libraries and data visualization elements in vector format
  • Easy-to-use Essential Graphics controls for rapid customization

How to Download and Install

  1. Browse the template collection and choose the one that fits your project needs.
  2. Click the download button and save the AEP or MOGRT file to your computer.
  3. Open Adobe After Effects and import the template using File > Open Project.
  4. Locate the Essential Graphics panel to customize colors, text, and data values.
  5. Render your final infographic using the built-in Adobe Media Encoder presets.

Choosing the Best Template for Your Project

Consider the story you want to tell and the data you need to present. A corporate quarterly report might benefit from a clean bar chart template with animated counters, while a social media campaign could use a bold icon-based infographic with dynamic transitions.

File format matters too — AEP files give you full control over every layer and effect, while MOGRT files are optimized for use within Premiere Pro without the need to open After Effects separately.

System Requirements

  • Adobe After Effects CC 2020 or later (some templates may require newer versions).
  • Minimum 8GB RAM (16GB+ recommended for complex scenes).
  • Graphics card with at least 2GB VRAM for smooth preview playback.
  • Sufficient disk space — templates typically range from 50MB to 500MB.
