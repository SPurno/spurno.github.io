Infographics Templates

📅 Feb 14, 2024 • 👁️ 70K+ Views • 📝 SPurno Animation Studio
✦ Buy From Adobe Stock

Infographics templates give you a head start on creating professional data visualizations and animated infographics. Pre-built layouts, charts, and transitions let you focus on content rather than starting from scratch.

Why Use Infographics Templates

Infographics templates save time and ensure consistent quality across your projects. Instead of building every element from the ground up, you can customize pre-designed templates that already follow best practices for visual hierarchy, color theory, and motion design. This approach dramatically reduces production time while maintaining professional standards that would otherwise require years of design experience to achieve consistently.

The business case for using infographics templates is compelling. Professional animated infographics can cost anywhere from $500 to $5,000 when custom-produced by design agencies. Template-based production reduces this cost by up to 90% while delivering comparable visual quality. For content teams producing infographics on a weekly or monthly basis, the cumulative savings quickly add up to significant amounts.

Templates are ideal for content creators, marketers, educators, and businesses who need to produce infographics regularly. They provide a repeatable framework that can be adapted for different data sets, brand identities, and distribution channels. A single well-designed template can serve as the foundation for dozens or even hundreds of individual infographic projects, each customized to present different data while maintaining consistent brand presentation.

SPurno's infographics templates are built with modularity in mind. Each template includes multiple scene options, adjustable color palettes, and flexible layout configurations that can be mixed and matched to create unique presentations every time. This modular approach means you never have to use a template exactly as-is — you can combine elements from different templates, add your own branding, and create truly custom results using our professionally designed building blocks.

Template Categories

  • Statistical Charts — Bar graphs, pie charts, line graphs with animated transitions
  • Timeline Infographics — Horizontal and vertical timeline layouts with sequential reveals
  • Comparison Slides — Side-by-side layouts for before/after, pros/cons, and feature comparisons
  • Process Flowcharts — Step-by-step animated process diagrams with connecting arrows

How to Customize a Template

  1. Download your chosen template in After Effects format (AEP) or motion graphics template (MOGRT).
  2. Open the template and locate the Essential Graphics panel for easy customization options.
  3. Replace placeholder text, colors, and data values with your own content and brand assets.
  4. Adjust animation timing and easing to match your desired pace and tone.
  5. Preview the result and export in your preferred format — MP4, MOV, or GIF.

Choosing the Right Template

Consider the type of data you are presenting and the platform where the infographic will be shown. A complex statistical report may need a detailed chart-based template, while a simple social media post might work best with a clean icon-based layout.

Always check the template resolution — 1080p is standard for web and social media, while 4K is recommended for broadcast and large displays. Also verify that the template includes editable color controls and supports your language for text overlays.

Pro Tips for Template Customization

  • Maintain brand consistency — update colors, fonts, and logos to match your identity.
  • Keep it simple — avoid overcrowding the template with too many data points.
  • Use contrast wisely — highlight key statistics with brighter colors or larger sizes.
  • Test on mobile — ensure text remains readable on smaller screens.
