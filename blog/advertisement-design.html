Why Advertise with SPurno

Our audience demographics reveal that 65% of SPurno visitors are professional motion designers and video editors with 3+ years of industry experience. Another 20% are agency owners and creative directors who make purchasing decisions for their teams. The remaining 15% are aspiring creators and students who represent the next generation of creative professionals — making them valuable for brand-building campaigns targeting future industry leaders.

Seasonal trends within the creative industry also work in advertisers' favor. Traffic peaks during Q4 (October–December) when creators produce holiday content, and again in Q1 (January–March) when new projects and campaigns launch. Advertisers who lock in annual or quarterly placements benefit from priority positioning during these high-traffic periods without rate increases.
